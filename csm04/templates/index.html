How does it work?

  • Paste link to analyze

    Begin by pasting the link of the product you wish to evaluate. Our website will swiftly process the provided URL to extract and scrutinize the associated reviews.

  • Get a review report

    Our system meticulously analyzes the reviews associated with the product. Within moments, you'll receive a comprehensive report highlighting key insights and trends extracted from the reviews.

  • Visualize & Understand

    Dive into the report with ease as it's presented with intuitive visualizations. Our clear and concise graphics make it simple to grasp the analysis findings, allowing you to discern genuine feedback from potentially misleading or fake reviews.
